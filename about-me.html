About Me

Who I Am

I'm Dallas Lucy — a creative based out of [your city], working across film, music, photography, and web development. I make things that feel intentional: visuals that carry weight, sounds that sit in the room with you, and code that doesn't get in the way of the idea.

My work as a filmmaker started in school, where I wrote and directed my first short. Music has been a longer thread — producing beats, building DJ sets, and releasing under the name Clouted Mar. On the technical side, I build and design websites, with an eye for dark, detail-forward aesthetics inspired by anime and lo-fi culture.

Everything I make is connected by the same thing: a refusal to be boring.
